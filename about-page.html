About Karnavati University

As part of the Karnavati University community, we are proud to share our story. Established in 2017 in Uvarsad, near Gandhinagar, Gujarat, we embarked on a mission to offer high-quality education across various disciplines. Our university was founded under The Gujarat Private Universities (Amendment) Act, 2017, by the Karnavati Medical and Educational Trust, marking the beginning of our journey towards academic excellence and innovation.

We are recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and are members of the Association of Commonwealth Universities (ACU), underscoring our commitment to maintaining high educational standards and fostering a global perspective. Our offerings span undergraduate and postgraduate programmes in fields such as management, design, law, liberal arts, commerce, media studies, and dentistry, facilitated through our six constituent colleges. These programmes are meticulously designed to equip our students with the necessary skills and knowledge to thrive in their careers.

Why Choose Karnavati University

Innovation in education is at the heart of our ethos. We leverage state-of-the-art academic infrastructure and foster collaborative partnerships with top-class universities and institutions worldwide. Our curriculum is enriched with opportunities for externships, guest lectures, certification courses, and industry visits, ensuring a comprehensive and experiential learning experience for our students. Accessibility to quality education for all students is a cornerstone of our philosophy. We offer scholarships to deserving candidates, aiming to support a diverse range of backgrounds and economic statuses. Our campus is equipped with modern facilities, including libraries, cafeterias, classrooms, and labs, all designed to support and enhance the student learning experience. We place a strong emphasis on career readiness and employability, with our dedicated global placement team attracting over 200 companies annually to offer a plethora of employment opportunities to our students across various sectors. At Karnavati University, we are united in our mission to foster an environment of learning, innovation, and success, preparing our students to be future leaders and changemakers in their respective fields.
